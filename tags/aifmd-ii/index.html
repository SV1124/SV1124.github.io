On 24 January 2023, the EP’s Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs (ECON) approved its amended draft (compromise) text of the amendments to the AIFMD and UCITSD (with 54 votes against 3, only the left-radical ‘The Left’ fraction voted against). The EP’s report (the “Final Report”) was published on 2 February 2023 and is available here. The Final Report follows a November 2021 proposal of the EC, an initial May 2022 draft report of the EP ECON Committee (the “May Draft”) and a June 2022 ‘compromise text’ of the Council.
