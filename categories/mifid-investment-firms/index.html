Last month I published an article in a peer reviewed journal: "All over the place: ETS emission allowances in EU financial regulaton",TRV-RPS 2024/1, 5-28. In the article, I provide a general overview of rules and obligations applicable to market participants that wish to hold or trade allowances - taking into account the recent updates of the Emissions Trading System. This overview demonstrates that the common thread of allowances-specific rules is a plethora of information and reporting requirements.
