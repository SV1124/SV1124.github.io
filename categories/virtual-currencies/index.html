New regulation on marketing virtual currencies to Belgian consumers
  • March 17, 2023
  • #Virtual currencies  
  • #marketing  
  • #Belgian law  
Crypto-winter does not only seem to be consolidation time for companies involved. It also is for legislators. With MiCA(R) on its way, a new Belgian regulation on marketing virtual currencies to Belgian consumers was published in the Belgian Official Gazette this morning.[1] The regulation is based on three elements: compliance with minimum rules to ensure that the information in the advertisement is correct and not misleading; the mandatory inclusion of specific warnings in the advertisement; and the obligation to notify the FSMA in advance of mass campaigns.
